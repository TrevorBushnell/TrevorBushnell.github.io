#### About

When I started a new barbershop quartet made up of classically trained singers, I knew I wanted to write a chart to kick off our time as a group. If you're looking for a fun chart that has elements of the barbershop style while also pushing the boundaries of traditional harmony found in barbershop charts, this is perfect for you and your group!

Please note that this is not a contestable barbershop chart.
